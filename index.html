      About the Capstone Architecture

      This system was developed to provide robust, data-driven diabetes risk assessments. Rather than relying on a single algorithm, this application utilizes a Soft Voting Ensemble. It averages the predictive probabilities of two highly optimized models: a Gradient Boosting Classifier and an eXtreme Gradient Boosting (XGBoost) model.


      Prior to inference, inputs undergo real-time feature engineering (e.g., BMI × Age, Glucose/Insulin ratios) and standard scaling to exactly mirror the training environment. This architecture ensures high diagnostic reliability, achieving a baseline accuracy of 83.1%.
